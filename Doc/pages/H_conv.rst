Converged Results
=================
In MD a large number of parameters must be chosen to ensure
publication quality results are obtained. Some of these include the size of the MD
box, the time step and the simulation length. The choice of these parameters
also depend on the analysis result, for example, the dynamics coherent
structure factor is much more difficult to obtain converged results
when compared to the dynamics incoherent structure factor.
